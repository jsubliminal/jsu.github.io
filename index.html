About Me (and the app!)

hello! i'm jesua, a 2nd year comsci student (eugh) from UPD who is currently trying to kickstart an app that is primarily a productivity app with its unique quirks :] it originally started as a personal project as an app that i designed to cater to me, but my friends said why not try and share this amalgamation of an app to the world... so.. hello world!

the purpose of this site is to find willing testers to help me catch bugs, improve on some features, and gain feedback before (trying) to launch this app full-swing!
